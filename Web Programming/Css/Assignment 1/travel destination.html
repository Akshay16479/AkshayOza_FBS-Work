Manali – The Valley of Mountains

Manali is a breathtaking hill station located in the northern state of Himachal Pradesh, India. Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, Manali is famous for its snow-covered mountains, lush green valleys, and scenic rivers.

Why Visit Manali?

Tourists visit Manali to enjoy adventure activities such as river rafting, paragliding, skiing, and trekking.

Popular Attractions

Famous places like Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, Hidimba Temple, and Old Manali showcase the natural beauty of the region.

Food and Culture

Manali is also known for its cozy cafes, traditional Himachali food, and warm hospitality.
